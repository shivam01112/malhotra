About Us

Precision Engineering Excellence

Precision-Engineered Solutions

Malhotra Engineering specializes in the manufacturing of hydraulic brake parts, rubber-to-metal bonded components, and precision rubber parts designed to meet the demanding performance requirements of modern vehicles. With strong technical capabilities and a commitment to innovation, the company delivers durable and high-performance solutions across domestic and international markets.

The company offers a comprehensive range of components for heavy-duty commercial vehicles, trailers, earthmoving equipment, material handling systems, and agricultural vehicles, supporting Indian, European, Japanese, Chinese, and American applications.
